# Word Popping — Support & Legal

**Word Popping** is a word-association puzzle game for iPhone: pop bubbles,
match words to categories, and clear the board.

## Support

Questions, feedback, or a problem with the game? Email us and we'll get back
to you:

**[robert.aneszko@gmail.com](mailto:robert.aneszko@gmail.com)**

When reporting a bug, it helps to include your iPhone model, iOS version,
and the level you were playing.

### Common questions

**Is my progress backed up?**
Progress is stored only on your device. Deleting the app deletes your
progress; it does not transfer between devices.

**Why am I seeing ads?**
Word Popping is free and ad-supported. All ads are non-personalized — see
the Privacy Policy for details.
